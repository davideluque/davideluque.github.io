About Analytics

Analytics is a web platform that provides customers direct access to consulting reports.


Administrators and clients can log in and recover their password via a link sent to their email.

Administrators register customers in the administrative area.

The reports are pdf files previously generated by the administrators. They are uploaded to the database in the administrative area.

Clients can access the reports through different filters such as sector, year and month of generation and type.


Note: some information may be hidden to protect the integrity of the product.

Technical information

  • Frontend: Adapted admin template.
  • Backend: Python 3 & Django.
  • Database: Django ORM & PostgreSQL.
  • Hosted in AWS Elastic Beanstalk.
