About me

My story

I was born in the small town Valera in the beautiful country of Venezuela.

Since I was a little kid, I always had passion for computers. I was interested to know how they worked both at software and hardware level.

As time passed, I became the computer technician of my mom's company; reinstalling operating systems and configuring computers with programs to optimize the work in the company. When I was not doing my job as a technician, I found myself downloading versions of games (Tibia, Habbo) and analyzing how they worked to make my own versions.

I was always struck by the development of web pages. In fact, the first programming language I learned before entering the university was PHP. I also used editors of online websites well known in my time as miarroba, palimpalem, among many others.

The knowledge I have today in programming I owe to my university and I want to continue keeping up with the latest software development technologies and continue studying to become a better Computer Engineer.
